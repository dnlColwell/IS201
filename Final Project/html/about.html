About Me

Just a guy who likes Jazz

Hometown | Family | Music

Raised in the wonderful town of Great Falls, MT, I grew up thinking I was in a large community only to learn quickly how small my city was. Montana is a beautiful place and it made me gain a love for the outdoors and the wonders of nature. I rock climb, hike, and kayak often just to get outdoors and experience all the world has to offer.
Come Visit!

I am the youngest of five siblings, and the son of two wonderful parents. I was spoiled as a kid with so many brothers and sisters to play with. They teased me, challenged me, and helped me learn to love. My parents of course did the same. Now a days all my siblings are married, and I don't see them as often as I might like, however that's more than made up for by all the nephews (no nieces mind you, none of my sisters or brothers seem to like having daughters).

Something that has always been incredibly influencial in my life is music. I am a trained Jazz pianist, vocalist, and trombonist. I have played ever since I was young and I have enjoyed it for about as long. Music gives me an opportunity to express my inner thoughts and desires, while simultaneously releasing stress that builds up throughout life. Jazz in particular allows for a freedom no other music provides. It is growth, purpose, and creation all in one, and I love it.
